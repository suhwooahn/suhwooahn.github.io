I am an assistant professor at the School of Media and Advertising at Kookmin University in Seoul. I completed my PhD in Communication at Michigan State University and was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Minnesota.

My primary research interest is how the media shape attitudes and beliefs about the political world, for better or for worse.

If you want to know more about me, please email me anytime at suhwooahn@kookmin.ac.kr.
