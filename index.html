I am a postdoctoral research associate in the Minnesota Journalism Center at the Hubbard School of Journalism and Mass Communication, University of Minnesota. I completed my PhD in Communication at Michigan State University.

My primary research interest is how the media shape attitudes and beliefs about the political world, for better or for worse.
